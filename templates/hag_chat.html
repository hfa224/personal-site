Hags Horror Summer

This month we've seen Maxxxine and Longlegs

I've been thinking about horror hype - how a film can never be as scary as you want or as good as you want. Maxxine's neon 80s dream is ultimately unsatisfying because she's not a bad enough bitch. Longlegs could never live up to it's hype, although I found it's beige detective noir satanic panic creepy wacky thrills fun enough.

Texas Chain Saw Massacre

I'd never seen this film and they were playing it at the old IMAX so I had to go - on a sunny hot day. There was a disappointing turn-out. We had a talk beforehand, There's so many iconic shots in this film and it's so bleak, but there's also a reading of it as a dark comedy - just like life I guess!
